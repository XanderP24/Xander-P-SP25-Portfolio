Animation

Breakthrough

The emotion that I decided to animate is triumph, more specifically ‘breakthrough’. I chose the red square because it was the first color and shape that came to mind for this project; he is also red, emphasizing the breakthrough emotion even further. I chose the day to go dark to represent hesitation and the doubt before the triumph. This was done with Adobe After Effects.
